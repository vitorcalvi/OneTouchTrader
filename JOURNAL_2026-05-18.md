## 📜 Rules Learned/Reinforced Today

### The QQQ Regime Rule
> QQQ direction determines INTC bias 90%+ of the time.
> **Never long INTC against QQQ. Never short INTC against QQQ.**
> Exception: clean volume-confirmed divergence — and even then, half size.
> Kill switch: if QQQ reverses your way during a trade, exit immediately regardless of INTC price.

### The Setup Quality Rule
> A trade needs THREE things to fire:
> 1. Regime aligned (QQQ direction)
> 2. Structure clear (level to fail/break, defined stop)
> 3. R:R ≥ 2:1 with realistic target
> Missing any one = no trade. No exceptions.

### The Patience Rule
> The market gives 5-10 setups per session. You need 1-2 winners.
> Sitting out a candle costs nothing. Forcing a trade costs everything.
> "I missed the move" is rarely true. The next setup is forming.

### The First-Signal Rule (NEW today)
> On trending days, the perfect pullback doesn't come.
> First confirmation = enter with reduced size (5K).
> Pullback if it comes = add (10K-15K).
> This solves "I waited for the perfect entry that never appeared."

### The Exit Discipline Rule
> Pre-define T1 and T2 BEFORE entering.
> Move to BE after +0.5R unrealized.
> Take 50% off at T1, runner to T2 with structural trail.
> Don't trail on every candle — trail on every structural shift.

### The Tooling Rule
> Stops should match the instrument's noise band, not arbitrary %.
> INTC intraday ATR (5min) ≈ $0.50-0.80. Stop at 0.75% = $0.80 = correct.
> If you're getting stopped on normal noise, your tool is wrong, not your timing.

### The Lunch Rule
> 11:30 - 13:30 = no-trade zone unless A+ setup at major level.
> Volume dies. Algos take over. Signals become noise.
> Walk away from the screen. Come back at 13:30 for the afternoon trend.
